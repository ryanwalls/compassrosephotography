FAQ

What is Birds of a Feather?
Birds of a Feather is an umbrella company for 4 photographers who share a similar vision and approach to weddings. We are better together.

Do you travel for weddings and portrait sessions?
Yes Yes Yes! We love traveling and being inspired by new locales, we’ll go wherever you invite us.

How much of an investment are we talking about here?
Each photographer has different pricing, with coverage starting at $3500. Please get in touch to find out more!

What’s involved in booking one of you for our wedding?
To book your date a contract and 50% deposit are required. We book on a first come, first serve basis, on receipt of a signed contract and 50% deposit.

Do you do engagement sessions, day-after sessions, rehearsal dinners and all of the other fun stuff we may want?
Yup. You name it, it’s probably on our price list (contact us to get one!). If there’s something you want that’s not on there – let us know and we’ll figure out if we can do it.

What kind of equipment do you use?
We currently use Canon professional gear. We love it pretty hard.

Do you shoot film or digital?
Sarah, Jen and Jacqueline are strictly digital and Rebecca shoots a mixture of film/digital.

Who will photograph our wedding with you?
We have a handful of second photographers that work with all of us. They’re professional, talented, and awesome – obviously.

Will we get the high resolution files (the digital negatives) to keep?
Heck yes. On your one year anniversary we will mail you a delightful little present that includes your digital negatives.

How long will it take for you to work your magic on our photos and post them online?
It takes around 2 months after your wedding date to post the photos. We’re all extremely picky about making sure the color is juuusssttt right, so going through the 3,000 + photos we took at a wedding takes some time.

What kinds of albums do you offer?
We offer Renaissance albums and proof books that are just too cute for words. If you’re out of state or can’t meet up to see them just email us and we’ll send you a pdf with pictures/info!

Do you have recommendations for what to wear to our engagement or portrait session?
Yes. This is something we’ll talk about when we set up a date/location. We’re here to help!

Do you have insurance?
Yes, we are covered by a comprehensive plan designed specifically for wedding and portrait photographers.

We love your work but we have specific ideas about what we want. Will you work with us?
Absolutely. Our goal is to make sure that you get the perfect photos that represent you and your style.

Why is the sky blue?
A clear day-time sky is blue because molecules in the air scatter blue light from the sun more than they scatter red light.
